# Privacy Notice — Reef Tycoon

_Last updated: 2026-04-29_

Reef Tycoon ("the experience", "we") is a Roblox experience operated by
groupsmix. This notice describes what data we collect from players and how
we use it. Roblox Corporation operates the platform you connect through;
their [Privacy Policy](https://en.help.roblox.com/hc/en-us/articles/115004630823)
governs the underlying account, age verification, parental consent, and
chat moderation.

## What we store inside the experience

We store the following per-player data in Roblox DataStores under your
Roblox UserId:

- Cash, gems, lifetime cash, prestige level, and tank slots (in-game economy)
- Inventory of fish you have pulled
- Tanks you have placed and the fish assigned to each
- Gacha counters (rolls since last Mythic / Epic) for pity timers
- Tutorial completion flag and daily-reward streak
- Last-seen timestamp (for offline earnings)
- Audit log entries on Mythic+ pulls and on flagged anti-cheat events

We do **not** store your real name, email, IP address, payment
information, chat messages, or any data Roblox does not already provide
to the experience under the standard Roblox API.

## Robux purchases

In-experience purchases (gamepasses, developer products) are processed by
Roblox. We receive a `ProductId` and a `PurchaseId` and apply the matching
in-game effect to your account. We do not see your payment details. All
refunds and chargebacks are handled by Roblox.

## Anti-cheat audit log

When the server detects abnormal cash gain rates, we record the event in
a Roblox DataStore so a human moderator can review it. The record includes
your UserId, the time, and the suspicious delta.

## Children

This experience may be played by users under 13. We do not knowingly
collect any personal information beyond what Roblox provides. Roblox is
COPPA-compliant for under-13 accounts.

## Off-platform contact (Discord, support)

If you join our Discord or contact support by email:

- Discord stores your handle, server roles, and any messages you send. We
  read these to support you and run live ops.
- Support emails are read by the developer and stored in the developer's
  email inbox. We do not forward them to anyone.

## Data deletion

Email a deletion request to the address listed in the experience's
description. We will delete your DataStore profile within 30 days. Roblox
may retain its own copies under their policy.

## Changes to this notice

We will update this file in the public repository when the policy
changes. The "Last updated" date above always reflects the current
version.
